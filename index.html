about

Kevin Singles is a cartoonist and designer based in Austin, TX.

He is the artist for the forthcoming graphic novel Head Games from First Second Books, with Les McClane and Craig McDonald, which will be released October 2017. He is also the creator of the upcoming self-published Unseen Genius.
